## Trere origins (Mafsoleios and the Gilded Hwispian Maw)

Per `core/setting/geography.md`'s Trere entry: three empires ruled Trere
before the Sundering. **Mafsoleios** and the **Gilded Hwispian Maw** are the
two that left recognizable heirs today - both nations' modern forms trace
back to Trere's fallen dynasties. The third empire is lost to memory, its
remnants said to linger between the Southlands and Trere - see
`kb/world/geography.md`, Trere detail.

The Gilded Hwispian Maw's side of this is now written into
`core/setting/nations.md` and `geography.md` (2026-07-20): their capital,
**Svarnanagaram**, is the old imperial seat of their Trere-empire
predecessor, inherited whole rather than built, and deliberately kept
blunter and less esoteric than the Chorus of Murmurs' Aegislith Veil.
**Mafsoleios' own entry still doesn't state its Trere-dynasty origin
explicitly** - that half of this item remains open, worth a small addition
next time Mafsoleios' page is revisited.

## Relations (402 RC)

Full matrix lives in `core/setting/nations.md` - reproduce it here only if
it needs to be edited alongside a lore change; otherwise link back rather
than risk two copies drifting. Headline read: the Hwispian Maw is at war
with almost everyone (Empire, Mafsoleios, Alashar, Desert Nomads); the
Orkhaden Horde is at war with the three major settled powers (Empire,
Coalition, Triumvirate) simultaneously; the Tagarian Coalition and Greater
Groves are at war with each other despite the Coalition otherwise being
broadly friendly. Update `kb/timeline/timeline.md` first when a relation
changes, then reconcile this file and canon together.
