# World Vocab

Domain-specific proper nouns for nations/factions/currencies/languages/
geography. Cross-cutting terms (cosmology, eras, dating) live in the root
`kb/vocab.md` instead.

## Nations

Holy Reino Empire, the Greater Groves, the Tor'Az Triumvirate, the Orkhaden
Horde, the Alashar Tribes, the Tagarian Coalition, the Chorus of Murmurs,
the Gilded Hwispian Maw, the Mafsoleios City-State, the Ederani Al'Nur /
Desert Nomads.

## Factions/orders

The Portatores, the Black Blades (Greater Groves - *to be written*), the
Donati ("il Datore" = their leader's title), the Golden Choir, the
Fellowship of Saint Cadogan, the Pirate Lords of Pearling Bay.

## Currencies

- **Crowns & Scales** - Dwergaz (Tor'Az). Brass Crowns, silver-cored;
  Scales worth 1/100th of a Crown.
- **Renos & Ferrins** - Holy Reino Empire. Silver Reno, copper Ferrin.
- **Mar Scrips** - Tagarian Coalition. Paper currency, magically sealed
  against forgery/water damage.

## Languages

- **Plethic** - common tongue of trade/diplomacy, fluid/adaptive.
- **Tagaraal** - Feliids.
- **Khazduul** - Dwergaz.
- **Aurkhani** - Aelves, confined mostly to sacred groves.
- **Batakluhk** - human, flowing/liquid consonants.
- **Mafari** - human, scholarly/philosophical.
- **Zakhrafan** - human, desert.
- **Grazn** - Skeggs (clicking, tunnel-dwelling).
- **Khorian** - Orkh battle-shouts.
- **Dzinariel** - Dzinari, riddle-speech.
- **Daemonic** - "dangerous," whispers corruption.
- **Dragon's Script** - ancient, shifts meaning based on the reader; found
  in ruins predating the mortal races.

## Geography proper nouns

See `kb/world/geography.md` for continents/locations - not duplicated here
to avoid a second list to keep in sync.

A few names worth flagging so they're not confused across continents:

- **The Sawolleas Sea** - separates Inggaz and Terrevault. Not adjacent to
  Trere or Lustralis, despite an earlier draft mixing this up - see
  `kb/world/geography.md`, Trere detail.
- **The South Seas** - separates Trere from the Southlands.
- **Noskirk Colony** - a Holy Reino Empire penal colony near the Sawolleas
  Sea (Inggaz/Terrevault region), not on Trere. Needs its own writeup
  whenever that region gets a detail pass - logged in `kb/issues/`.
