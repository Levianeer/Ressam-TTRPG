# Vocab (cross-cutting)

Invented terms and proper nouns that cut across multiple `kb/` domains.
Domain-specific proper nouns (nation/faction/currency/language names) live
in `kb/world/vocab.md` instead - don't duplicate them here.

- **Ressam** - the world/setting.
- **the Mourning Star** - Ressam's sun; orbits the planet, not vice versa.
  See `kb/canon/canon.md`.
- **Patéras and Mitéra** - the twin moons ("the Father and the Mother").
  See `kb/canon/canon.md`.
- **Reino Concordium (RC)** - the dating system; Third Age begins at 1 RC.
  Always give era + RC year for datable events.
- **the Age of Mythos** - the mythic creation era, before RC dating.
- **Thryzzaral** - the first God; fractured mind birthed the universe.
- **the Great Artists / the Four Painters** - same beings, two names used by
  different cultures (`history.md` vs. the Elar'laevien faith). Use
  whichever name matches the POV/culture of the text being written.
- **the God-Dragons** (Infierno, Rocas, Tempeste, Cascate) - elemental
  dragons, entropy's opponents, dormant since the Sundering.
- **the Sundering** - the calamity that ends the Age of Mythos and sets the
  RC calendar's zero point; Aldric Grimthorn's banishment of the God-Dragons
  and its fallout.
- **the Fe Alada / Fe Aladan Faith** - the God-Dragon-worshipping religion
  founded in secret by Tempeste; now the Holy Reino Empire's state religion.
- **the Age of Revival** - the mistrustful recovery era between the
  Sundering and the start of RC dating.
- **the Third Age** - the current era, 1-402 RC and counting.
- **"mortal god"** - an in-world term of art (used explicitly for the
  Devaraja and Sir Davil), not just a `kb/` organizing category - see
  `kb/characters/mortal-gods.md`.

Expand this file as new cross-cutting lore is drafted; add domain-specific
terms to `kb/world/vocab.md` instead of here.
