Character creation in Ressam builds your character through deliberate choices, not random rolls or class restrictions. Every decision shapes who your character is and what they can do.

## Step 1: Starting Experience

Set **EXP to 100** (Level 1). You gain a level every 100 EXP.

---

## Step 2: Choose Race

See the **Races** chapter. Record:

- Attribute modifiers (applied after point allocation in Step 4\)
- Starting languages
- Racial abilities
- Movement speed
- Special senses (darkvision, low-light vision)

---

## Step 3: Choose Career

See the **Careers** chapter. Record:

- Skill modifiers (free; doesn't count against your skill point budget)
- Starting equipment
- Starting currency

---

## Step 4: Distribute Attribute Points

You have **36 points** to distribute across 8 Attributes.

### **Attribute Generation Methods**

| Method | Rules |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Point Buy (Default)** | Distribute **36** points. |
| **Standard Array** | Assign **7, 6, 5, 5, 4, 4, 3, 2** to Attributes. Each value is used once. |
| **Rolled** | For each **8** attributes, roll **3d6**, **divide by 3** (round to nearest whole) and **add 1**. You can assign these results freely between each attribute. |

**Note:** No Attribute can start **below 2** before racial modifiers, **below 1** after, or **above 8** after racial modifiers.

### 

### **Attribute Limits**

| Level | Maximum (before racial mods) |
| :---: | :---: |
| 1-3 | 7 |
| 4-7 | 8 |
| 8-11 | 9 |
| 12+ | 10 |

**Minimum:** No Attribute below 2 before racial modifiers, or below 1 after.

**Racial increases/decreases** are applied after distributing points to your modifier.

---

## Step 5: Distribute Skill Points

You have **24 Skill points** to distribute. Career skill bonuses are free and applied first.

### **Skill Limits**

| Level | Maximum Rank |
| :---: | :---: |
| 1 | 3 |
| 2 | 4 |
| 3 | 5 |
| ... | Level \+ 2 |
| 8+ | 10 (cap) |

---

## Step 6: Choose Starting Feats

You receive **2 Feats** at character creation.

- You must meet all prerequisites
- Some feats can be taken multiple times (noted in description)
- Racial and Career skill modifiers count toward prerequisites

See the **Feats** chapter for the complete list.

---

## Step 7: Calculate Derived Stats

| Stat | Formula |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Hit Points (HP)** | (END × 3\) \+ 10 |
| **Mana Points (MP)** | MIND × 2 |
| **Carrying Capacity** | ((END \+ STR) × 10\) \+ 10 lbs |
| **Evasion** | 5 \+ Agility \+ DEX − Armor Penalty |
| **Dodge** | Evasion \+ 5 |
| **Max Dodges/Short Rest** | DEX ÷ 2 |
| **Initiative** | 1d12 \+ ((REF \+ DEX) ÷ 2\) |
| **Armor Rating (AR)** | Equipped armor's AR value |

---

## Step 8: Choose Equipment

Your career provides starting equipment and currency. Purchase additional items from the **Equipment** chapter.

**Armor Reminder:** Armor Penalty affects Evasion, Dodge, and spellcasting. Each 2 ranks in Armorer reduces that penalty by 1\.

---

## Step 9: Final Details

- **Name:** Appropriate to race and setting
- **Appearance:** Age, build, distinguishing features
- **Personality:** At least one core trait, one flaw, one bond
- **Goals:** Short-term and long-term
- **Connections:** How you know the party, why you're adventuring
