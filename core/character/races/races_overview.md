### **General Rules Notes**

- **Natural Weapons:** Count as unarmed weapons for all purposes (feats, magical enhancement, etc.)  
- **Weapon Training:** Racial Skill and Attribute bonuses count toward feat prerequisites  
- **Size Modifiers:** See Core Rules for Slot capacity and space requirements by size

---

### **Level Adjustment**

Some races have power beyond standard racial traits, represented by Level Adjustment (LA). Your LA counts toward your total level but does not grant skill points, attributes, or other progression benefits.

**Example:** A LA \+2 character at total level 5 has the progression of a level 3 character, plus their racial features.

## Races

### **Artefolk**

The four common races of Ressam: Aelves, Dwergaz, Humans, and Orkhs.

### **Cursed**

Races born of divine wrath or cursed: Alsahli, Feliids, Skeggs, Tembels, and Khoridae.

### **Mystik**

Beings willed or dreamed into existence rather than born: Dzinari, Golems, and Tapio.

### **Night-Terror**

Nightmares given form, spawned from mortal fear: Daemons, Varulf, and Strygs.
