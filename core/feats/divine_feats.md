These feats represent deeper divine connection and are available only to Divine spellcasters.

---

## Divine Specialization

### **Divine Focus**

Your prayers to one aspect of the divine are especially powerful.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in any Divine school  
**Benefit:** Choose one Divine school. When you Pray, you may choose one spell from that school to be usable twice before needing to Pray again.  
**Special:** You may select this feat multiple times. Each time it applies to a different Divine school.

---

### **Devoted Servant**

Your faith allows you to commune with the divine more quickly.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in any Divine school  
**Benefit:** You may Pray as a 30-minute activity instead of 1 hour. Additionally, when you complete a Pray, regain HP equal to 1d4.

---

### **Empowered Prayers**

Your devotion strengthens the potency of your divine invocations.

**Prerequisites:** \+5 ranks in any Divine school, FAI 5+  
**Benefit:** When you Pray, all spells you prepare from Divine schools gain a \+1 bonus to all spell rolls until your next Prayer.  
**Special:** You may select this feat multiple times. Each time, the bonus increases by \+1 (maximum \+3).

---

### **Channel Divinity**

You can channel raw divine power into miraculous effects.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in any Divine school, FAI 6+  
**Benefit:** Once per Field Rest or Long Rest, you may channel raw divine energy as a Major Action. Choose one effect:

1. **Divine Healing:** Grant 2d8 temporary HP to all allies within 30 ft
2. **Divine Blessing:** All allies within 30 ft gain advantage on their next attack or Ward
3. **Divine Wrath:** Make a spell attack against one creature within 60 ft for 3d6 \+ FAI radiant damage
4. **Divine Protection:** All allies within 30 ft gain \+3 AR for 1 minute

---

## Divine Defense

### **Divine Resistance**

Your faith shields you from hostile divine magic.

**Prerequisites:** \+3 ranks in Religious Lore  
**Benefit:** Divine spell rolls against you have disadvantage.

---

## School-Specific Divine Feats

### **Beacon of Hope**

Your presence inspires and protects your allies.

**Prerequisites:** \+6 ranks in any Divine school  
**Benefit:** When you cast a Divine spell that affects allies, they gain \+1 to all Wards for 1 minute (this stacks up to a maximum of \+3).

---

### **Ward of Faith**

You can invoke a protective barrier infused with divine power.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in any Divine school, FAI 5+  
**Benefit:** As a Reaction when you or an ally within 30 ft takes damage from an attack or spell, you may expend 2 Mana to grant the target resistance to that damage type for the attack (halving the damage). You can use this feature a number of times per Field Rest or Long Rest equal to your FAI ÷ 3 (minimum 1).

---

### **Unyielding Devotion**

Your resolve in faith makes you harder to sway or harm through supernatural means.

**Prerequisites:** \+3 ranks in Religious Lore, FAI 4+  
**Benefit:** You gain \+2 to all Wards against effects that would charm, frighten, or possess you. If you succeed on such a Ward, you regain 1d6 HP as divine favor rewards your steadfastness.

---

### **Wrath of the Faithful**

You can channel divine fury into devastating attacks.

**Prerequisites:** \+6 ranks in Benediction, FAI 7+  
**Benefit:** Once per Field Rest or Long Rest, enter a state of divine fervor for 1 minute (Major Action). While in this state: your Benediction spells deal an additional 1d8 radiant damage, you have advantage on attack rolls against creatures that have damaged your allies, and you gain temporary HP equal to your FAI.

---

### **Nature's Wrath**

Your natural magic is especially difficult to resist.

**Prerequisites:** \+6 ranks in Cultivation  
**Benefit:** When you cast a Cultivation spell that deals damage, you have advantage on spell overcome rolls for that spell. Creatures you overcome also have their movement speed reduced by 10 ft until the end of their next turn.
