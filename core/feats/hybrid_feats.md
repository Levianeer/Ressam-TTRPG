## Magical Combat

*These feats blend martial prowess with magical ability, requiring training in both weapon skills and spellcasting.*

### **Mystik Knight**

You have learned to weave magic into your defensive techniques.

**Prerequisites:** \+5 ranks in Shields Skill, \+5 ranks in a magic Skill  
**Benefit:** When you take the Block Reaction, you may cast a spell with a Mana Cost of 2 or less for free. Resolve the attack first, then the spell's effects. Effects must target or hit only the attacking creature. Your spell overcome rolls made through this feature have disadvantage and any damage dealt is halved.

---

### **Spell Strike**

You can channel spells through your weapon strikes.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in a weapon Skill, \+4 ranks in a magic Skill  
**Benefit:** Once per turn, when making a weapon attack, you may cast a spell (costs \+3 Mana Cost extra, or \+2 once you have \+6 ranks in both the weapon Skill and magic Skill used) with the same action. The spell must target or hit only the creature you hit with its area of effect.

---

### **Battle Mage**

You have mastered the art of casting in armor.

**Prerequisites:** \+3 ranks in Armorer, \+3 ranks in a magic Skill  
**Benefit:** You have learned to cast in armor through drilling and practice. Your equipped Armor's Penalty is reduced by 2 (stacking with the Armorer Skill) when making spell rolls. Additionally, you do not need a free hand to cast spells (you use your weapon hand).

---

### **Matter over Mind**

You’ve learnt the Mafsoleian art of manipulating the arcane with your spirit alone.

**Prerequisites:** REF 6+, \+4 ranks in a finesse weapon Skill, \+2 ranks in a magic Skill  
**Benefit:** You may use the Parry Maneuver against spell attacks. When a spell that makes a spell attack roll would hit you, and you can see the caster, you may Parry it as though it were a melee attack: make a weapon attack roll, and if it equals or exceeds the spell's attack roll, roll your weapon's damage and reduce the spell's damage by that amount.

**Note:** This works only against single-target spells that make a spell attack roll (against your Evasion) \- not spells that force a Ward (spell overcome rolls), area-of-effect spells, or a caster you cannot see. As with a normal Parry, a critical spell attack bypasses it, and you must have a finesse melee weapon in hand.
