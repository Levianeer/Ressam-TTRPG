These feats represent advanced study of Arcane magic and are available only to Arcane spellcasters.

---

## Metamagic

*These feats allow Arcane spellcasters to modify their spells in powerful ways.*

### **Metamagic Initiate**

You have learned to modify your spells while casting.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in any Arcane school, MIND 5+  
**Benefit:** Learn one metamagic option from the following list. You can apply this metamagic when casting scribed spells from any Arcane school you know:

- **Empower Spell:** When you cast a spell that deals damage, increase all damage dice by one size (d6→d8, d8→d10, d10→d12). Costs \+3 Mana.
- **Widen Spell:** When you cast an area-of-effect spell, you may widen it to double its radius/area. Costs \+2 Mana to cast.
- **Lengthen Spell:** When you cast a spell with a duration, you may increase it to double its max duration. Costs \+2 Mana to cast.
- **Silent Spell:** When you cast a spell, you may make it silent. Costs \+2 Mana to cast.

**Special:** This is the first feat in the Metamagic chain.

---

### **Metamagic Adept**

Your understanding of spell modification deepens.

**Prerequisites:** Metamagic Initiate, \+3 ranks in two Arcane schools, MIND 5+  
**Benefit:** Learn two additional metamagic options from the Metamagic Initiate list.  
**Special:** This is part of the Metamagic chain.

---

### **Metamagic Mastery**

You have perfected one metamagic technique.

**Prerequisites:** Metamagic Adept, \+6 ranks in any Arcane school, MIND 6+  
**Benefit:** The Mana Cost of all metamagics you know have their cost reduced by 1 (minimum 1).  
**Special:** This is part of the Metamagic chain.

## Arcane Specialization

### **Arcane Focus**

You have specialized in one school of Arcane magic.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in any Arcane school  
**Benefit:** Choose one Arcane school. When you scribe a scroll from your chosen school, reduce its Mana cost by 1 (minimum 1). This reduction applies after calculating the spell's final cost.  
**Special:** You may select this feat multiple times. Each time it applies to a different Arcane school.

---

### **Spell Penetration**

Your spells break through magical defenses with ease.

**Prerequisites:** \+6 ranks in any Arcane school  
**Benefit:** When you hit an enemy with a spell attack and cause their armor to degrade, their armor loses 1 additional AR beyond normal (2 total per hit instead of 1). Additionally you gain \+1 to all spell rolls.

---

### **War Caster**

You have trained to maintain spells even in the chaos of battle.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in any Arcane school  
**Benefit:** You have advantage on MIND Wards to maintain concentration on spells. When a creature provokes an opportunity attack from you, you may cast a spell targeting them instead of making a melee attack.

---

### **Focused Channeling**

You can maintain channeled spells even when wounded.

**Prerequisites:** MIND 7+  
**Benefit:** When you fail the MIND Ward check to maintain a channeled spell after taking damage, you may immediately spend Mana Points equal to the spell's Mana Cost to continue the spell anyway.

---

### **Spell Sculptor**

You can shape your area spells to protect your allies.

**Prerequisites:** \+6 ranks in any Arcane school, MIND 7+  
**Benefit:** When you cast a spell that affects an area, you may choose a number of creatures equal to your MIND. The spell automatically fails to overcome those creatures and they take no damage.

---

## Arcane Defense

### **Arcane Resistance**

Your study of magic has made you resistant to its effects.

**Prerequisites:** \+3 ranks in Arcane Lore  
**Benefit:** Arcane spell rolls against you have disadvantage.  
**Special:** This is the first feat in the Arcane Resistance chain.

---

### **Arcane Devourer**

You can consume hostile magic and turn it against your enemies.

**Prerequisites:** Arcane Resistance, \+5 ranks in Arcane Lore  
**Benefit:** When a creature within 60 ft casts a spell you can see, you may use your Reaction to nullify its magic. This action costs Mana equal to the spell's Mana Cost \+ 1\. The spell is countered and has no effect.  
**Special:** This is part of the Arcane Resistance chain.

---

### **Arcane Barrier**

You can weave protective magic at a moment's notice.

**Prerequisites:** \+5 ranks in any Arcane school, ARC 6+  
**Benefit:** As a Reaction when you would be targeted by an attack, you may spend 2 Mana Points to gain \+5 AR against that attack. This magical armor lasts only for that single attack.

---

### **Arcane Recovery**

You can meditate to recover magical energy between battles.

**Prerequisites:** \+4 ranks in Arcane Lore, MIND 6+  
**Benefit:** Once per Field Rest or Long Rest, during a Short Rest, you may spend 30 minutes in meditation to recover Mana Points equal to half your Arcane Lore Skill ranks (rounded down, maximum \= half your MIND score). Mana regained through Arcane Recovery can be used to scribe scrolls immediately.

---

## Elemental Magic

### **Elemental Affinity**

Your body has adapted to your chosen element.

**Prerequisites:** \+5 ranks in Aeromancy, Geomancy, Hydromancy, or Pyromancy  
**Benefit:** Choose one elemental school you have \+5 ranks in. You gain resistance to that element's damage type (slashing for Aeromancy, fire for Pyromancy, cold for Hydromancy, bludgeoning for Geomancy).  
**Special:** You may select this feat multiple times. Each time it applies to a different elemental school.

---

### **Elemental Mastery**

Your elemental magic pierces resistances and immunities.

**Prerequisites:** \+6 ranks in Geomancy, Hydromancy, or Pyromancy  
**Benefit:** Choose one elemental school you have at least \+6 ranks in. Creatures with resistance to your spell's damage type take full damage (as if they had no resistance). Creatures with immunity to your spell's damage type are treated as if they had resistance instead (taking half damage).  
**Special:** You may select this feat multiple times, applying it to different schools each time.
