Ressam as a system is based around overland travel that is measured in hexes, with each hex being **six miles** across or **two leagues**. Hex points are spent to travel through hexes. On foot, collectively, the party has a base hex point allowance of **10 per day** (representing **10 hours** of travel). Some factors can reduce or increase a character’s point allowance. Mounts directly modify the party’s daily hex points, if each party member is actively riding the mounts.

| Hex Terrain Types | Hex Entry Cost |
| :---- | :---: |
| Plains / steppe / farmland | 4 |
| Hills / woods / desert / rough | 6 |
| Mountains / jungle / swamps | 8 |

| Hex Terrain Modifiers | Cost Modifier |
| :---- | :---: |
| Heavy rain / deep snow / thick fog | \+2 |
| Temperature extremes | \+2 |
| Good roads / excellent trails | -2 |

### **Entering a Hex**

If a party wants to enter a hex but lacks some of the points/hours needed, the points/hours they spend count toward progress, but the party ends that segment in their current hex. The party only enters the new hex once its full hex point cost is paid (progress resets for the next hex).

- Leftover points/hours from one day carry over as progress toward the next hex.  
- Mounts: If all party members are mounted, replace the party’s daily point max with the mount's daily point max. Unmounted members slow the party back to foot speed.

### **Cautious March**

The party slows their pace to better spot and avoid alerting potential enemies. Reduce the party’s daily hex point allowance to 6 (6 hours max). However:

- The party cannot be surprised.  
- If ambushed, attackers have disadvantage on initiative rolls.  
- Time spent: Still 1 hour per point, but capped lower daily.

### **Forced March**

The party pushes beyond normal limits. Add \+4 to their daily hex point allowance, but all characters gain 2 levels of Exhaustion at day’s end.

- Such a pace can be kept up until a marcher reaches 6 levels of Exhaustion.  
- Most Followers or Cohorts so fatigued will press for at least one full rest day; refusal may provoke Morale checks.  
- If ambushed, the party is surprised.

### **Rations on the Road**

Each full travel day consumes **1 ration per character**, on top of the Field Long Rest ration cost (see Core Rules, Food and Water). Traveling Clothes' "consume rations every 2 days instead of daily" benefit is an exception to this rule, not a separate one.

## Mount Stats

### **MULE**

*Hardy pack animal, stubborn and reliable*

| Stat | Value |
| :---- | :---- |
| **HP** | 25 |
| **Evasion** | 8 |
| **AR** | 1 (thick hide) |
| **Speed** | 40 ft |
| **Daily Hex Pts** | 10 |
| **Attributes** | STR 5, END 6, DEX 3, REF 4 |

**Attacks:**

- **Kick:** \+3 to hit, 1d6+2 bludgeoning damage (only when spooked or cornered)

**Special Traits:**

- **Beast of Burden:** Can carry 12 Slots without penalty  
- **Sure-Footed:** Advantage on Wards against being knocked prone  
- **Stubborn:** Disadvantage on Animal Handling checks to make it do something dangerous

**Behavior:** Won't willingly enter combat. Requires DC 15 Animal Handling to prevent fleeing from violence.

### **RIDING HORSE**

*Swift travel mount, not trained for battle*

| Stat | Value |
| :---- | :---- |
| **HP** | 35 |
| **Evasion** | 11 |
| **AR** | 2 |
| **Speed** | 70 ft |
| **Daily Hex Pts** | 12 |
| **Attributes** | STR 6, END 6, DEX 6, REF 5 |

**Attacks:**

- **Hooves:** \+4 to hit, 1d8+3 bludgeoning damage (only when panicked, or if you travelled at least 30ft before attacking a target medium or smaller)

**Special Traits:**

- **Beast of Burden:** Can carry 12 Slots without penalty  
- **Fleet-Footed:** Can Dash as a Minor Action  
- **Skittish:** When taking 10+ damage, must make DC 13 Animal Handling check or panic (moves randomly, rider must make DEX Ward DC 20 or fall)

**Behavior:** Flees from combat unless controlled (DC 12 Animal Handling each round). Not trained to fight.

### **WAR HORSE**

*Battle-trained destrier, bred for combat*

| Stat | Value |
| :---- | :---- |
| **HP** | 55 |
| **Evasion** | 10 |
| **AR** | 3 (can wear barding for \+3 AR) |
| **Speed** | 60 ft |
| **Daily Hex Pts** | 12 |
| **Attributes** | STR 8, END 7, DEX 5, REF 4 |

**Attacks:**

- **Hooves:** \+6 to hit, 2d6+4 bludgeoning damage

**Special Traits:**

- **Beast of Burden:** Can carry 15 Slots without penalty  
- **Warhorse Training:** Automatically acts on rider's initiative. Can attack without Animal Handling checks  
- **Fearless Charge:** If the horse moves at least 30 ft in a straight line before attacking, deals an additional 2d6 damage and target must make STR Ward (DC 14\) or be knocked prone  
- **Battle Hardened:** Advantage on Wards against fear and being startled. Only panics when reduced below half HP

**Behavior:** Trained for combat. Will attack enemies on command and defend its rider. Requires DC 10 Animal Handling if reduced below half HP to prevent fleeing.
