## Revelry & Celebrations

When your party reaches safety after a deadly mission, you can celebrate together \- drinking, feasting, gambling, or reveling in ways that reaffirm you're still alive. Pool your coin, share stories of your exploits, and convert your coin into experience through hard-won lessons learned in cups and conversation.

**Requirements:**

- Must be in a settlement with appropriate venues (taverns, feast halls, gambling dens)  
- All participating characters pool their coin together  
- Each participant rolls individually on the Results table  
- The celebration takes place over the time listed

**How It Works:**

- Players decide which tier of celebration to fund  
- Pool coin together to meet the total cost  
- Each player rolls 1d6 \+ the celebration tier's bonus  
- Consult the Results table for outcomes  
- Time passes based on celebration tier

### **CELEBRATION TIERS**

| Tier: | Total Cost: | Time: | Bonus: | Description: |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---- |
| **Average Day Off** | 50 Crown | 1 night | 0 | Cheap wine and tall tales in a common room |
| **Modest Drinks** | 100 Crown | 1 day | \+1 | Decent ale, warm food, a bit of music |
| **Respectable Night** | 300 Crown | 2 day | \+2 | Private table, good food, gambling, entertainment |
| **Proper Revelry** | 600 Crown | 3 days | \+3 | Quality wines, hired musicians, multiple venues |
| **Grand Celebration** | 900 Crown | 4 days | \+4 | Fine wines, exotic dishes, storytellers, private rooms |
| **Lavish Feast** | 1,200 Crown | 5 days | \+5 | Tavern buyout, renowned performers, excess and spectacle |
| **Legendary Banquet** | 2,000 Crown | 1 week | \+6 | A legendary week of drinking and celebration, attracting hundreds of guests |

### **RESULTS TABLE**

| Result: | XP Gain: | Description: |
| :---: | :---: | :---- |
| **1** | 1 XP | **Wasted Night.** You drank too much, too fast. Wake up with a splitting headache and vague shame. Lost 1d6 Crown to pickpockets or "generous" impulses you don't remember. |
| **2** | 2 XP | **Hazy Memories.** The night's a blur of bad decisions. You recall fragments \- a card game you lost, an argument you started, someone's name you've already forgotten. At least you learned something about your limits. |
| **3** | 2 XP | **Modest Gains.** Pleasant enough evening, but nothing profound. Swapped a few stories, made small talk with locals, and went to bed at a reasonable hour. Safe, boring, marginally educational. |
| **4** | 5 XP | **Good Company.** Shared drinks with fellow survivors. Their war stories mirror yours \- the fear, the near-misses, the weight of it all. You're not alone in this. That's worth something. |
| **5** | 5 XP | **Lucky Streak.** Won a few hands at cards, bought a round for the house, basked in fleeting glory. The crowd's cheers reminded you why you fight: moments like these, however brief. |
| **6** | 10 XP | **Tales of Glory.** Recounted your recent exploits to a captive audience. In the retelling, you understood the choices you made \- what worked, what nearly killed you. Storytelling as debriefing. |
| **7** | 10 XP | **Valuable Contact.** Met someone useful: a merchant, a veteran, a fellow traveler. Exchanged information, made a connection. Gain one local contact (DM determines details). |
| **8** | 15 XP | **Crucible Moment.** A brawl broke out \- maybe you started it, maybe you ended it. Either way, you learned something about yourself under pressure. Gain advantage on your next Intimidation or Athletics check. |
| **9** | 15 XP | **Wisdom in Wine.** Deep conversation with a stranger \- philosophy, faith, mortality. They challenged your assumptions. You left the table changed, even if you can't articulate how. |
| **10** | 20 XP | **Legendary Night.** Everything aligned. Perfect company, perfect timing, perfect insight. You'll remember this night for years. These are the moments that make survival worthwhile. |
| **11** | 25 XP | **Epiphany.** Somewhere between the third drink and dawn, clarity struck. A pattern you'd missed, a connection you hadn't seen. You understand your craft \- or yourself \- in a new way. |
| **12** | 25 XP | **The Story They'll Tell.** You did something extraordinary \- won an impossible wager, spoke truth to power, earned the respect of hardened killers. Your name will be spoken in this place for months. Gain local Reputation (DM determines mechanical benefit). |
