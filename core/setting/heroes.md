## Aldric Grimthorn

A Human master wizard, the only one capable of long-range teleportation. A contested legacy, as he is said to be deeply linked to the God-Dragons and their destruction, but also someone who helped stop their wrath. Some worship him and the mercenaries he hired as heroes for saving the realm, while many believe he's irredeemable.

## Voa Shadowhorn

A legendary wizard who played an important role in stopping the Sundering. Though devastated by the loss of her comrades during the cataclysm, she eventually found solace in her arcane studies. Voa became a world-renowned master of 'Shadow Magic,' using her powers to manipulate shadows and darkness to her will. Her legacy lives on in the mystical arts she pioneered and the students she mentored.

## Alfred Sibbell Hunter

A legendary Human Night-Terror hunter, one of only a few to survive a journey to the Fields of Nocturne.

## God-King Khor

A once Orkh warlord, who after banding together all of the great Orkh tribes during the 2nd Era, ascended to godhood, worshipped among Orkhs as their saviour.

## Ghorzuk, Firemaw

An incredibly intelligent Orkh, known for his uniquely un-Orkh-like cunning. He is also the current "Champion," a title granted by the God-King Khor to only the most capable of his followers.

## Master Ozah Bale

The oldest known living Tembel. He has grown to nearly 10 feet tall and is almost entirely immobile. Many visit Ozah on a pilgrimage, often to seek his council. But his most common guests are his students attending to his health.

## Durnir, the Ironhand

The first of the Dwergan Triumvirate. Not much is known about Durnir, only that he runs the Barazel guild, the wealthiest and largest guild in the Tor'Az. Many have questions about him, but most that ask have been answered with missing people and a trail of corpses.

## Runvar, the Unshakable

The second of the Dwergan Triumvirate. Runvar is a warrior of great renown, having earned his title when he led a successful campaign against the largest Tierratuar clan of the time, slaying their near god-like leader. He now leads the Matgadel guild.

## Dwarin, the Industrious

The third of the Dwergan Triumvirate. Leader of the Dromgadol guild, a Dwergan exclave near the heart of Orkhen territory. The constant conflict with the Orkh Horde and their proximity to the Infernal Gate has led to unconventional innovations. Known for their experiments on captured Orkhs and Daemons, they gave rise to the Tierratuar race as a weapon to pit against their enemies.

## Torin Elmbrow

A ranger known for his unparalleled tracking and survival skills. He has spent his life protecting the wilderness and its inhabitants from those who would exploit or destroy it. Torin is a master of stealth and camouflage, often appearing when least expected to defend the natural world.

## Thalorien of the Greater Groves

Thalorien is the wise and enigmatic leader of the Greater Groves, a council of united Aelfen groves. Known for his deep understanding of ancient lore and magic, Thalorien has guided his people through countless challenges. He is a symbol of wisdom and resilience, embodying the enduring spirit of the Aelfen race. Thalorien rose to prominence during the Sundering, using his knowledge and diplomacy to unite the Aelfen groves. His leadership has been instrumental in maintaining peace and prosperity among his people.

## High Lord Victor Ardan

The ruler of the Holy Reino Empire and the head of the Fe Alada religion. Known for his unyielding determination and strategic brilliance, Victor has led his empire to greatness through a combination of military might and political acumen. He is revered by his followers and feared by his enemies. Victor's rise to power was marked by his ability to unite disparate factions under a common banner. His leadership has expanded the influence of the Holy Reino Empire across Ressam. Victor's policies and military strategies have shaped the political landscape of Ressam, making him one of the most influential figures in recent history.

## Vespertine the Gilded

A mysterious Dzinari known for her radiant beauty and enigmatic presence. She is said to possess incredible magical abilities and is often associated with light and illumination. Vespertine's true origins and motives remain shrouded in mystery, adding to her allure and intrigue. Vespertine appeared suddenly, her origins shrouded in mystery. She has used her powers to aid those in need, often appearing as a beacon of hope in dark times.

## The Koté

Prince of the Tagarian Coalition, figurehead of the Council of Sages, known as a young, spoiled brat but a genius with an incredible charm and wit. Few among the aristocrats respect him due to his age and inexperience, however his talent and ambition may yet wrest the council to his reign.

## The Devaraja

An ancient Dzinari who has discovered the universe's most fundamental secrets \- laws that govern all reality. Though he possesses the knowledge to heal the world's slowly fading divine immunity, he instead uses this power to forge chains of oppression. He manifests horrors like Deapsang, an addictive drug that ensures perfect slavery, and commands the Golden Choir \- elite warriors surgically stripped of all capacity for expression. Constrained by cosmic forces that would erase him if he pushes reality too far, he plays a careful game of manufactured consent, making the world believe his tyranny is earned rather than stolen. The ultimate paradox: the one mortal with the power to save everyone, who chooses instead to damn them all.
