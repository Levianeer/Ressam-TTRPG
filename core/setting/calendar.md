Ressam's calendar follows the eternal dance of its twin moons through four seasonal quarters, each split into two months. Citizens mark their days by **Gaudivivo** (spring renewal), **Irafoco** (summer's fire), **Tristemara** (autumn's harvest), and **Pavoreombra** (winter's reflection). At the same time, the moons' crossings serve as celestial clockwork for a world still learning to measure time since the dragons fell.

---

## Gaudivivo - Spring

### Albavivo - The First Half - 45 Days

* **First Light (1st).** Celebrates the renewal of life and the first day of spring.
* **The Highsunn (27th).** Celebrates the liberation of Pearling Bay by Sir Davil the God of Thieves, from the Tagarian Coalition, marking the city's rebirth under the banners of freedom, self-reliance, and self-rule.
* **Verdant Bloom (32nd).** A Greater Groves festival with planting ceremonies and floral displays.

### Florivivo - The Second Half - 46 Days

* **Day of Providence (59th).** Honours divine blessings and gratitude for the year's new beginnings.
* **Ascension of Crafts (84th).** A Dwergaz day of crafting competitions and exhibitions.

---

## Irafoco - Summer

### Ascenfoco - The First Half - 48 Days

* **Eternal Ember (27th).** An Orkhish and Khoridae holiday with fiery displays and ritual combat.

### Ardenfoco - The Second Half - 46 Days

* **Ocean's Bounty (51st).** Celebrates maritime communities with feasts and ship races.
* **Day of Storms (78th).** A Dzinari festival of rituals harnessing summer winds and exchanging secrets.

---

## Tristemara - Autumn

### Velamara - The First Half - 44 Days

* **Veil's Opening (15th).** Honors the connection between the material and divine planes, observed by Mystikal races.

### Colhamara - The Second Half - 43 Days

* **Hearthheart (45th).** A human tradition commemorating alliances formed during the Sundering.
* **Harvest Festival (65th).** A celebration of the end of harvest season with markets, dances, and bonfires.

---

## Pavoreombra - Winter

### Geliombra - The First Half - 47 Days

* **Whispered Solace (26th).** A Dzinari day of reflection on secrets shared and kept, with oaths for the new year.

### Noctombra - The Second Half - 46 Days

* **Eternal Vigil (57th).** A Tembel tradition marking the longest night with meditation and continuous fire.
* **Day of Revelry (90th).** A universal celebration welcoming the new year with music, dance, and fireworks.
