Spell Crafting is an alternative to the normal spells, meant as a better expression of a mage that the normal spell list may lack. It, however, is meant to be a flavoured investment, not a cheap way to gain power or break interactions.

**Requirements:**

- **Minimum Skill Rank 3** in the relevant magic school  
- **Arcane:** Access to research materials (spellbooks, grimoires, alchemical texts)  
- **Divine:** Theological texts and understanding of your deity's domain

**Learning Curve:** Your first few custom spells will likely be inefficient. Mastery comes from experimentation and refinement.

---

## The Three-Part Formula

Every spell follows the same fundamental structure:

**VERB \+ NOUN \+ MODIFIERS \= SPELL**

- **VERB:** What does the spell *do*?  
- **NOUN:** What does it *affect*?  
- **MODIFIERS:** How is it *delivered*?

---

## Point Budget

**Base Points \= Spell Crafting Skill \+ Noun's associated Magic School Skill**

**Building a Spell:**

1. Choose your VERB(S)  \-  spend points  
2. Choose your NOUN(S)  \-  spend points  
3. Apply the MODIFIERS  \-  add or subtract points  
4. Calculate Mana Cost: **4 \- (Remaining Points ÷ 4\)**, minimum 1

Your spell's final Mana Cost cannot be lower than the Magnitude's minimum.

---

## VERBS (The Action)

All verbs include conjuration as needed \- you pay for the effect, not whether the material already exists.

| Verb | Cost | Effect |
| :---- | :---: | :---- |
| **Detect** | 1 | Sense or divine information  \-  no physical effect |
| **Project** | 1 | Create sensory illusion  \-  false perception, no substance |
| **Wield** | 2 | Attack, shape, move, or create the element |
| **Bind** | 3 | Seal, ward, protect, or contain  \-  defensive magic only |

---

## NOUNS (The Element)

Cost depends on your skill rank in the relevant school:

| Skill Rank | 3 | 6 | 8 | 10 |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Point Cost** | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 |

| School | Noun |
| :---- | :---- |
| Aeromancy | Air/Wind |
| Geomancy | Earth/Stone |
| Hydromancy | Water/Ice |
| Pyromancy | Fire/Heat |
| Shadowmancy | Shadow/Darkness |
| Benediction | Wrath/Judgment |
| Cultivation | Nature/Body |
| Invocation | Soul/Familiar |
| Necration | Death/Decay |
| Subjugation | Mind/Manipulation |

### 

### **Multi-School Spells**

When combining Nouns from multiple schools:

- Use your *lowest* school skill for Budget calculation  
- Pay each Noun's cost separately  
- Each school beyond the first costs \+2 points

## MODIFIERS

### **Range**

| Range | Adjustment | Distance |
| :---- | :---: | :---- |
| **Self/Touch** | \+2 | Physical contact (cannot have area modifier) |
| **Close** | \+1 | 10 ft |
| **Near** | 0 | 30 ft (baseline) |
| **Medium** | \-4 | 60 ft |
| **Far** | \-8 | 90 ft |
| **Distant** | \-12 | 120 ft |

### 

### **Duration**

| Duration | Adjustment | Notes |
| :---- | :---: | :---- |
| **Instant** | 0 | Effect resolves immediately |
| **1 Round** | \-2 | 6 seconds |
| **1 Minute** | \-4 | 10 rounds |
| **Channel (1 min)** | \-2 | Requires concentration (see Channeling rules) |

### 

### **Area**

| Area | Adjustment |
| :---- | :---: |
| **Single Target** | 0 |
| **5 ft radius** | \-2 |
| **10 ft radius** | \-4 |

### **Magnitude**

| Magnitude | Adjustment | Damage | Min. Mana |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Cantrip** | \+4 | 1d4 \+ ARC/FAI | 1 |
| **Weak** | \+2 | 1d6 \+ ARC/FAI | 1 |

| **Cataclysmic** | \-6 | 6d8 \+ ARC/FAI | 5 |

**Non-Damage Effects:** Use DM discretion for potency scaling.

---

## Crafting in Play

Custom spells must be **learned** before use. Both Arcane and Divine casters spend a Long Rest to permanently add the spell to their repertoire (using the standard Learning Spells rules: 2 hours and 50 Crown per Mana Cost).

Once learned:

- **Arcane casters** scribe the spell onto scrolls like any other spell  
- **Divine casters** cast it spontaneously like any other known spell
