Arcane magic is the science of energy manipulation. Through rigorous study, you learn to recognize the patterns the God-Dragons left in the world \- the way fire always rises, water always flows, earth always grounds. By understanding these fundamental laws, you can reshape energy into controlled effects.

The elemental schools (Aeromancy, Geomancy, Pyromancy, Hydromancy) are taught first because they follow the clearest, most stable patterns. Shadowmancy is an advanced study \- manipulating absence and dissolution.

**For core mechanics (Scribing, Instability, Mana), see The Nature of Magic.**

### **Why Scribing?**

Arcane magic requires precision \- every symbol, every line of power precisely inked. This is why arcane casters carry scrolls, ink and notes, need quiet time each morning, and why a ruined spellbook is a disaster.

**The Advantage:** Your spells are tangible. Hand a Searing Heat scroll to your warrior companion. Sell them, trade them, stockpile them. Magic becomes a commodity.

**The Disadvantage:** Lose your scrolls and you're defenseless. Get ambushed before morning scribing? You have no magic ready.
