Subjugation practitioners understand that faith is fuel, not a gift \- and fuel can be stolen. Based upon ancient Dzinari technique that is still not fully understood, Subjugation petitions no god; it siphons the raw conviction that prayer generates, wherever that conviction can be found \- scraps of another's devotion, a martyr's dying oath, or, most efficiently and most illegally, torn directly from a living mind mid-thought. Where other Divine schools channel their own faith outward, students of Subjugation reach into a victim's and turn it back against them \- which is why its spells contest a target's FAI Ward rather than their MIND: the attack lands on their conviction, not their reasoning. The Fe Alada officially denounces Subjugation as "manipulation of the divine gift of free will," yet a small number of their own Inquisitors secretly study it. None embody this parasitism more completely than its greatest practitioner, the Highest Devaraja of the Golden Throne \- whose own claim to godhood remains bitterly disputed.

### **SPELLS**

**Mental Lancet \- Mana Cost 1**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** 60 ft
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Effect:** Strike the target's mind with focused will. Make a spell overcome roll vs. the target's FAI Ward.

  - **Overcome:** Target takes 1d8+FAI psychic damage. If the target is channeling a spell, they have disadvantage on their concentration check.
  - **Resist:** Target takes 1d4+FAI psychic damage.

**Implanted Uncertainty \- Mana Cost 2**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** 30 ft
- **Duration:** 1 round
- **Effect:** You implant the vivid illusion of an imminent attack \- a blade at their throat, an arrow mid-flight, a beast lunging from shadow. Make a spell overcome roll vs. the target's FAI Ward.

  - **Overcome:** The phantom attack feels real. They take 2d6+FAI psychic damage and have disadvantage on attack rolls until the end of their next turn as they struggle to distinguish real threats from imagined ones.
  - **Resist:** They recognize the illusion, but the momentary flinch still costs them \- they take 1d6+FAI psychic damage.

**Commanding Whisper \- Mana Cost 3**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** 30 ft
- **Duration:** 1 round
- **Effect:** You plant an irresistible compulsion in a creature's mind disguised as their own impulse. Make a spell overcome roll vs. the target's FAI Ward.

  - **Overcome:** On their next turn, they must use their Major Action to perform a simple command of your choice: Approach (move toward a point you specify), Flee (move away from you), Drop (release whatever they're holding), or Grovel (fall prone and end their turn).
  - **Resist:** The target feels a strong urge but resists \- they have disadvantage on their first attack roll on their next turn as they fight the compulsion.


- The target will not follow a command that is directly self-destructive (walking off a cliff, attacking themselves). After completing the command, they are aware they were manipulated.

**Fracturing Will \- Mana Cost 3**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** 30 ft
- **Duration:** 10 rounds
- **Effect:** You burrow into a creature's decision-making, making every choice agonizing. Make a spell overcome roll vs. the target's FAI Ward.

  - **Overcome:** At the start of each of their turns, they must choose one:
    - Act Hesitantly, they are Stunned until the end of their turn.
    - Act Painfully, they take 1d8 \+FAI psychic damage.
  - **Resist:** The psychic pressure still rattles them \- they have disadvantage on their next attack roll as they shake off the mental intrusion.


- At the end of each of their turns, the target may attempt to reassert themselves by rolling 1d12 \+ FAI \+ best FAI skill vs. the original spell roll. On success, the effect ends.

**Constructed Perception \- Mana Cost 4**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** 60 ft (15 ft radius)
- **Duration:** Channel
- **Effect:** Create a convincing illusion that manifests in the minds of all creatures in the area. Make a spell overcome roll vs. each creature's MIND Ward.

  - **Overcome:** Creatures believe the illusion is real. You can create terrain, objects, or creatures. Physical interaction reveals the illusion, but believers rationalize inconsistencies. Believers attacking illusory enemies waste their actions. Damage from "illusory" sources feels real (e.g a creature believes they're being burned by illusory fire, they take 1d8 psychic damage). While channeling, you can alter the illusion (Minor Action).
  - **Resist:** The creatures see through the illusion but the sheer psychic weight of the attempt causes 1d8 psychic damage as the projected images fragment in their minds.

**Gaze of the Sharadhi \- Mana Cost 5**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** 30 ft (requires direct eye contact)
- **Duration:** Channel
- **Effect:** Lock eyes with a target who can see you. Make a spell overcome roll vs. the target's FAI Ward. On success, they become ensnared in your gaze. While the spell is active, apply ONE of these effects for the duration:

- **Overcome:**
  - **Predator's Gaze (Offensive):** You have advantage on attack rolls against the target. You can see through any illusions or disguises they're using


  - **Relentless Presence (Defensive):** Target has disadvantage on attack rolls against you. Target cannot benefit from cover or concealment against your attacks


  - **Dominating Stare (Control):** Once per round as a Reaction: Make a spell overcome roll vs. the target's MIND Ward. On success, their action fails (Mana lost, action wasted). Each time you use the Reaction, you take 1d6 psychic damage as the mental contest strains you.

- **Resist:** The creature sees through the illusion but the sheer psychic weight of the attempt causes 2d8 psychic damage as they wrench free of the gaze.
