Cultivation governs vitality itself \- its flow, transfer, and transformation. Cultivators understand that life and death are not opposites but a cycle: death feeds life, healing requires sacrifice, and the body can be coaxed to endure beyond its limits. It's the magic of those who understand nature's brutal honesty. Cultivators are simultaneously essential and unsettling. Villages welcome them for healing the sick and ensuring harvests, but watch nervously when they drain a dying animal to save a child. You're expected to heal freely and endlessly \- and resented when you can't. The Fe Alada tolerates Cultivation because it follows "natural law," but that tolerance is thin.

### **SPELLS**

**Thaumaturge \- Mana Cost 1**

- **Casting Time:** Minor Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Effect:** Channel vital energy into a wounded creature. The target gains 1d8+FAI temporary hit points. You may spend additional Mana to have the target gain 1d4 tHP per additional Mana.

**Parasitic Tendrils \- Mana Cost 2**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** 30 ft
- **Duration:** 1 minute
- **Effect:** Summon draining vines that latch onto a creature. Make a spell overcome roll vs. the target's STR Ward.

  - **Overcome:** The target's movement speed is reduced to 15 ft. At the start of their turn, the target takes 1d8+FAI necrotic damage. Choose yourself or an ally within 30 ft of the target to regain tHP equal to half the damage dealt \- the vines feed whoever you will, not just you.
  - **Resist:** The tendrils lash the target before falling away \- they take 1d8+FAI necrotic damage.


- They may use a Major Action to attempt to break free by rolling 1d12 \+ STR \+ best STR skill vs. the original spell roll.

**Ironwood Flesh \- Mana Cost 2**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Effect:** You channel vital energy into a creature's muscles and sinews, hardening them like living wood. For the duration, the target gains:

  - You gain \+2 to all Cultivation spell rolls.
  - You gain \+2 AR (does not degrade) and advantage on STR Wards


- When the spell ends, the target's muscles ache \- they have disadvantage on DEX checks and Wards for 10 minutes as their body readjusts.

**Arboreal Integument \- Mana Cost 3**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** Self (15 ft radius)
- **Duration:** Channel
- **Effect:** You become a wellspring of restorative energy. When you cast this spell and at the start of each of your turns while channeling, each ally within range gains 1d4+FAI temporary hit points.

**Respite from Ruin \- Mana Cost 4**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Effect:** You fortify a creature's body against accumulated trauma. The target gains 3d8+FAI temporary hit points. Additionally, if the target has any levels of Exhaustion, remove 1 level.

**Temporal Fortification \- Mana Cost 5**

- **Casting Time:** Major Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 10 rounds
- **Effect:** As a tree's layers may protect it from fire, you may channel divine power to layer a target's form against damage. The target gains 10 temporary END (this gains the target HP (END × 3 \= HP)). At the start of each of the target's turns, this bonus decreases by 1\. When the bonus reaches 0 or the spell ends, they immediately suffer 2 levels of Exhaustion.
